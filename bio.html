Hi! Nirmala Pillai Here

I write and paint. I have published stories and two volumes of poems in English. I have published a number of short stories in English in leading English magazine like India Literature of Sahitya Academy, Kavya Bharti, Asian Age, The Telegraph, PEN, the Little Magazine, Femina, Eve’s montly. The Heritage Magazine, Cha an Asian Journal from Hong Kong, Message in a poetry magazine U.K. etc.
(Nirmala's PROFILE)
​A self-taught artist uses mixed media. The concept behind my paintings is the response of individual to sensory perception of nature which is commonly overlooked like grass weds and flowers and their underlying beauty of delicate and bright colours in shapes and patterns. Faces of women are also captured in myriad fleeting expressions in colour. Colourbecomes an alphabet to express the world around me. ​Nirmala Pillai held painting exhibitions as follows :
